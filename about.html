About GreenSteps Community Gardens

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Cape Town, GreenSteps began as a neighbourhood garden project. It has grown into a non-profit organisation supporting over 50 community gardens across South Africa.

Vision: A future where every community has access to healthy, locally grown food.
