Garden Establishment

We help communities identify suitable land, conduct soil tests, and establish productive gardens from the ground up. Our team provides site preparation, irrigation setup, and initial planting guidance.

Skills Training Workshops

Monthly workshops covering composting, pest management, seasonal planting, and harvesting techniques. All training is conducted in local languages by certified instructors.

School Garden Program

Educational gardens in schools teach children about nutrition, environmental science, and food production while providing fresh vegetables for school feeding schemes.
